# Validation Plan: AI Adoption Last Mile

Hypothesis: `kizashi/hypotheses/ai-adoption-last-mile.md`
Last Updated: 2026-05-17

## Questions To Validate

- Why do AI PoCs fail after promising demos?
- Which adoption artifacts make implementation durable?
- Can the problem be narrowed to developer-agent adoption where Hachiware Labs has stronger fit?

## Evidence To Collect

- 5 examples of AI PoCs that did not become daily workflows.
- Interviews with AI tool vendors, customer success leads, and internal AI champions.
- Before/after workflow maps from one AI implementation.

## Search Queries

- `AI PoC failure workflow adoption`
- `forward deployed engineer AI adoption`
- `Copilot business impact measurement`
- `AI implementation last mile enterprise`

## Interview Questions

- What happened after the demo?
- Who owned the workflow change?
- What artifact would have prevented the PoC from dying?
- Did the failure come from model quality, integration, incentives, or process redesign?

## Adoption Criteria

- Buyers report a repeated need for implementation artifacts.
- A narrow developer-agent adoption playbook creates a concrete next action for one team.

## Rejection Criteria

- The work is mostly bespoke consulting with little reusable product surface.
- Hachiware Labs cannot narrow the wedge to developer workflows.

## MVP Experiment

Create a "PoC to Daily Workflow" playbook for one Codex or Copilot workflow and test it with a real team or solo builder.
